LegalSeagull

Analyze and visualize legal information.

Legislation

Legal Seagull supports the United States Code, with plans to add regulations, treaties, orders, and state law in the close future.

With LS, you can Integrate your research with Evernote-- select material and add it right to your notebook from the same screen.

Automatically generate citations while reading, and see cross-references to legal cases and other parts of the law created on the fly next to the reading area.

Searching is easy with our elegant search engine. No more need for complicated, repetitive menus.

Court Cases

Legal Seagull supports Supreme Court cases, and will shortly add appellate and state court decisions as well, later expanding to more broadly cover legal documents, present and historical.

Cases are presented in an easy-to-use timeline format, whether sorting generally, by topic, or by Justice, for instance browsing through a Justice's most-cited cases.

Also supports Evernote and automatically created citations.

See dissents and overrulings from the same page as the case.
